About Third Eye

Third Eye is an AI-powered forensic system that converts eyewitness descriptions into realistic suspect faces. It helps police departments create suspect sketches quickly, accurately and efficiently using Artificial Intelligence.

Project Objective

To assist police investigations by generating realistic suspect faces from eyewitness descriptions using Artificial Intelligence.
